Our Story

The Tayabas Community Hospital, Inc. was founded in May 1974 and began operations on June 1, 1976. It is governed by the Board of Directors, with Dr. Avelino Aguerra Obispo serving as Chairman and Medical Director.

The hospital is located in Brgy. Wakas, Tayabas, Quezon. It was established by Dr. Avelino A. Obispo and originally consisted of a main building, which was later followed by a three-storey Annex Building.
